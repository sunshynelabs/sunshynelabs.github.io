What Sunshyne Labs builds
Not generic “AI tools” — but focused systems that remove friction and preserve human bandwidth.
  • JACK – Tactical decision framework. A compact mental OS for operators who can’t afford analysis paralysis. Built as prompts, cards, and workflows.
  • GABRIEL – CX emotional buffer. A guardrail system that reduces second-hand trauma for agents by structuring how they handle difficult interactions.
  • MADISON – Workflow engine. A modular engine that turns messy, multi-step processes into predictable flows that tools (and humans) can follow.
  • ANGEL – Data entry & grid automation. Designed to kill repetitive, error-prone data work so people can focus on judgment and communication.
  • Example Corp sandboxes. Fictional but fully wired demo environments for major appliances support — safe to show, realistic to operate.
  • Custom lab builds. Systems built around real-world constraints: BPO, CX, ops, and AI orchestration.
