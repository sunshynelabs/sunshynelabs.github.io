Research
Sunshyne Labs builds systems that remove friction from real-world CX Ops environments where human entropy in legacy platforms corrode accuracy and client data integrity. Built from First Principles, these systems are designed to train humans calibrated towards both ends of the production pipeline—with machines, methods and frameworks made autonomous in the middle.
  • MARY – Compliance Matrix & Retention Engine
    A retention and de-escalation system for contact centers, built to keep customers, boost AI-native CX Vendor Ops & help mitigate client expenditure.
  • GABRIEL∞ – Autopoietic Knowledge Base
    A guardrail framework that reduces second-hand trauma and cognitive load for agents by structuring how difficult interactions are interpreted and answered.
  • MADISON – Brand Enhancement Engine
    A modular engine that turns messy, multi-step contact-center processes into predictable flows designed to positively influence client brand perception.
  • SANDRA – Data Entry Accelerator
    A data retrieval and structuring system designed to cut manual research and cleanup work down to a fraction of the usual time.
  • ANGEL – Data Entry Automaton
    A macro + AI hybrid framework that mimics repetitive, error-prone manual data work with uniform speed and consistency across spreadsheets and forms.
  • Octopus Corpus – AI Voice Architecture
    A conversational AI framework capable of orchestrating complex voice interaction analogues for CXOps call workflows.
  • The Liberators – Productivity & Automation Scripts
    A collection of high-impact scripts derived from the philosophy of ANGEL & SANDRA, built for superhuman productivity in CX and operations environments.
    • JACKΩ – Personal Self-Improvement Tool
      A “Prompt-In-A-Box™” decision framework that breaks analysis paralysis and forces clear action for high-performing individuals.
    • Superproxy – Client Voice Simulation Framework
      A lightweight system that helps replicate a client’s tone using text datasets and an LLM, killing blank-page friction without heavy tooling.
    • JOHN – Zero-shot Proposal Builder
      A rapid-response assistant for diagnosing client problems, drafting proposals, and applying just-in-time learning to real work.
    • JILLØ v1 – Cognitive Skill Accelerator
      A pocket-sized learning framework that turns any LLM into a structured tutor for fast, applied skill-building and problem-solving.
    • Example Corporation
      Sandbox environment for CX support, letting teams test AI workflows safely without touching real customer data.
