EC Model 1 – Technical Specifications

Appliance: EC Model 1 Electric Dryer
Brand: Example Corporation
Category: Residential Electric Clothes Dryer

Physical Specifications

Overall Height 38.7 in (98.3 cm)
Overall Width 27.0 in (68.6 cm)
Overall Depth 30.2 in (76.7 cm) excluding controls
Net Weight Approximately 120 lbs (54.5 kg)
Capacity 7.4 cu. ft.

Electrical Requirements

Power Supply 240V / 60 Hz
Recommended Circuit Dedicated 30-amp circuit
Plug Type 3-prong or 4-prong dryer outlet (depending on region/code)
Power Consumption (avg) Approx. 5,000 – 6,000 watts

Ventilation & Airflow

Exhaust Type Rear vented (convertible to side or bottom)
Recommended Duct Rigid or semi-rigid metal only
Maximum Duct Length ≤ 25 ft (7.6 m), reduced with each 90° bend
Airflow Monitoring Integrated safety sensing system

Control & Operation

Control Interface Electronic push-buttons + manual selector
Primary Modes Auto Dry, Timed Dry, Air Only
Temperature Options Low / Medium / High / Air Dry
Drum Material Galvanized steel

Integrated Safety Systems

Overheat Protection Automatic thermal cut-off and system shutdown
Airflow Restriction Detection D80 / D90 / D95 safety code (protective alerts)
Door Switch Safety Automatic stop on door opening

Environmental Conditions

Operating Temperature 5°C – 35°C (41°F – 95°F)
Installation Environment Dry, ventilated, indoors only
Usage Type Residential (non-commercial)

Compliance & Notes

  • Designed to meet standard residential electrical and ventilation safety requirements.
  • External vent restriction triggers protective shutdown to reduce fire risk.
  • Improper installation invalidates performance guarantees.
