EC MODEL 1 — LIMITED WARRANTY

Model: EC-M1ED  |  Product: EC Model 1 Electric Dryer

Brand: Example Corp (Creative Commons project of Sunshyne Labs)

1. Definitions

“Product” — The electric clothes dryer sold and labeled as EC Model 1 (EC-M1ED) by Example Corporation.

“Original Purchaser” — The individual who purchased the Product new, from an authorized source, for residential use.

“Residential Use” — Use of the Product in a single-family or multi-family household. Commercial, rental, shared, or industrial use is excluded unless otherwise stated in writing.

2. Warranty Coverage

Example Corporation warrants that the Product will be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal residential use, subject to the terms below.

Parts & Labor

1 year from the original date of purchase.

Covers:

  • Internal electronic components
  • Control panel and ExCortex board
  • Motor and heater assembly
  • Sensors and safety systems

Example Corporation may, at its discretion:

  • Repair the Product
  • Replace defective parts
  • Provide a replacement unit of equal or greater functionality

Drum (Parts Only)

5 years from the original date of purchase.

Covers defects in the dryer drum material or structure only. Labor is not included after the first year.

3. What Is Not Covered

This warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by improper installation or improper venting
  • Damage from blocked airflow or failure to clean vents and lint filter
  • Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents, discoloration)
  • Damage due to misuse, abuse, flooding, fire, or pests
  • Damage caused by modifications or unauthorized repairs
  • Damage from non-standard or unstable power supply
  • Normal wear and tear of consumable parts
  • SMS charges once any prepaid allotment is exhausted (where applicable)

CHECK VENTILATION alerts are treated as maintenance and environmental issues, not a product defect, unless proven otherwise.

4. Owner Responsibilities

To keep this warranty valid, the owner must:

  • Install the dryer according to local codes and the installation guide
  • Use a 4-inch metal exhaust duct (rigid or semi-rigid)
  • Keep the lint filter clean before or after each load
  • Maintain a clear and unobstructed vent path
  • Allow adequate airflow behind and around the unit
  • Prevent crushing, kinking, or excessive bending of the exhaust hose

Failure to follow these responsibilities may void this warranty.

5. How to Request Service

To initiate a warranty claim:

  1. Have your serial number ready
    (located behind the terminal block cover at the rear of the unit)
  2. Take note of:
    • Installation date
    • Description of the problem
    • Any messages displayed on the panel or received via SMS
  3. Contact Example Corporation:
    • Via SMS (if your number is registered with ExCortex), or
    • Via the support channel listed on your purchase documentation (future web form).

Repairs may require proof of purchase.

6. Limitations & Disclaimers

This warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser.

Example Corporation is not responsible for:

  • Loss of time or loss of use
  • Property damage, including water or fire damage
  • Incidental or consequential damages
  • Costs incurred by third-party services not authorized by Example Corporation

Some jurisdictions do not allow certain limitations on implied warranties or the exclusion of incidental or consequential damages. In such cases, some of the above limitations may not apply to you.

7. System Note — ExCortex

The ExCortex system is designed to operate with:

  • Offline telemetry only
  • SMS-only communication via integrated GSM module
  • No Wi-Fi, no cloud connection, and no browser or web access
  • No transmission of personal data to third parties

All diagnostics remain local to the machine. This design is intentional and part of the product’s safety and privacy model.
